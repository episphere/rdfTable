rdfTable [code]

Assembling RDF content from SOCRATA data platform as a FAIR linked table. Please provide the URL of the remote data as a link appended to the url of this page, as in this demo, which will retrieve the first 10 weeks of mortality data in 2021 (including COVID) from CDC. A second demo example looks at women under 30 years of age treated for Breast Cancer in 2017 in any care facility in Manhatan, by pulling data directly from New York state Department of Health. The URL for data sources can also be provided manualy in the input box below.
